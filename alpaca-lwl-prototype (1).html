Pass the Phone: Staff Connection Game

By Sarah Martinez | Updated March 10, 2026
[ Resource Image / Preview ]

Pass the Phone is a quick, lighthearted staff activity that sparks laughter while helping team members recognize the strengths and quirks that make their school community special. Inspired by the popular social media trend, this version keeps the focus on appreciation, positivity, and connection.

This activity works beautifully as a staff meeting energizer, end-of-week celebration, or team-building moment during professional development. The format is simple: staff members "pass the phone" to share quick, fun observations about their colleagues that build community and boost morale.

Whether you're looking to break the ice with a new team or strengthen bonds with veteran educators, Pass the Phone creates moments of recognition and joy that remind everyone why they love working together.
